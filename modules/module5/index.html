Vocabulary Bank

Cybersecurity

Protection of computers, devices, and data from unauthorized access or attacks.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Computer systems that can learn from data and make decisions or recommendations.

Ransomware

Malicious software that locks your files and demands money to unlock them.

Identity Theft

When someone steals your personal information and pretends to be you.

Privacy

Keeping your personal information safe and controlling who can see it.

Security Risks

Dangers that could let hackers access your information or devices.

Encryption

A way to scramble data so only authorized people can understand it.

Ethical Computing

Using technology fairly and responsibly, respecting people's rights and privacy.

Password

A secret word or phrase that protects your accounts from others.
